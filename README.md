# Confessing Her

A small personal web page I made with a simple idea in mind —  
a moving title on the screen and a countdown to **14 February 2036**.

Nothing complicated. Just HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

## What it does

The page shows the text **“Confessing Her”** bouncing around the screen like the old DVD logo screensavers.  
Along with it, there’s a countdown showing how many days are left until **Valentine’s Day 2036**.

There’s also background music playing while the page runs.

The whole thing is meant to feel a bit retro and minimal.

## Why I made this

Mostly just for fun and practice.

I like building small random projects, and this one is kind of a small personal idea.  
It is like a tiny digital reminder that some moments are worth waiting for.

## Tech used

No frameworks or libraries.

## Running it

Just open `index.html` in a browser.
